Dear Photography Lovers,
We are glad to inform you that, Film & Photography club is going to organize a platform for photography enthusiasts to express their talent & skills in photography.
Finally this semester, we are Organizing “The View Finder Season -6”. Students from all Departments(uapian’s) are cordially invited to the event.
Ex-Students & Faculty members from CSE departments also can participate. Your valuable participation will make our exhibition more meaningful.

Rules for Submission

# Each participant can submit a maximum of 9 photos.
# Submission will not be accepted without proper rename.
# Color and Black & White both will be accepted.
# Borders, Watermarks, signature, logo, Wedding Photos are strictly prohibited.
# Over-processed photographs will not be accepted.
# Manipulation will be accepted only for conceptual photos.
# Images should be submitted ".jpg" format at high-resolution.
No RAW images or TIFF images will be accepted. File size should not exceed 30-megapixels.
# Images should also be identified and dated, and include camera-recorded metadata.
# The photo must not contain material that violates or infringes another's rights, including but not limited to privacy, publicity or intellectual property rights, or anything that constitutes copyright infringement.
# The photo must, in its entirety, be a single work of original material taken by the contestant.
# The photo must not contain content that is inappropriate, indecent, obscene hateful, tortuous, defamatory, slanderous or libelous.
# The photo must not contain material that promotes bigotry, racism, hatred or harm against any group or individual or promotes discrimination based on race, sex, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation or age.
# The photo must not contain material that is unlawful, in violation of or contrary to the laws or regulations in any state where Photo was created. # The photo cannot have been submitted in any previous photography exhibition.
